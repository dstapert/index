AutoPress

AutoPress is an autoclicker/macro application made with WinForms and C#. The autoclicker has a ton of customization. Things you can customize are the interval, input type, hold interval, random relative position and random click timing to name a few. You can write out and/or record mouse/keyboard actions with the built-in macro editor and then save and play them.
